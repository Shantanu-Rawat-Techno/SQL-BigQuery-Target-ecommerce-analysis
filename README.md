# Target Brazil E-commerce Analysis (SQL | BigQuery)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a real-world e-commerce dataset from Target’s operations in Brazil. The dataset contains over 100,000 orders (2016–2018), covering customer behavior, payments, delivery performance, and product-level details.

The objective was to extract meaningful business insights and provide data-driven recommendations using SQL.

---

## Problem Statement

As a data analyst, the task was to explore and analyze Target’s Brazil dataset to understand:

* Order trends and seasonality
* Customer distribution
* Revenue and freight patterns
* Delivery performance
* Payment behavior

---

## Tools & Technologies

* SQL (Google BigQuery)
* Data Analysis
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

---

## Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Exploratory Data Analysis

* Checked dataset structure and data types
* Identified time range of orders (2016–2018)
* Analyzed geographic spread across states and cities

### 2. Order Trends & Seasonality

* Strong growth observed from 2016 to 2018
* Peak orders in late 2017 (holiday season effect)
* High monthly activity between May–August

### 3. Customer Behavior Insights

* Customers are widely distributed across 27 states
* Highest concentration in São Paulo (SP), Rio de Janeiro (RJ), and Minas Gerais (MG)

### 4. Revenue & Cost Analysis

* 129% increase in total order value (2017 → 2018)
* SP, RJ, and MG contribute the highest total revenue
* Smaller states show higher average order value

### 5. Freight & Logistics Analysis

* Higher freight costs in low-volume states
* Efficient logistics observed in high-demand regions (SP, PR, MG)

### 6. Delivery Performance

* Significant delays observed in some orders (200+ days)
* Faster delivery performance in high-volume states
* Some regions outperform estimated delivery timelines

### 7. Payment Analysis

* Credit card is the most preferred payment method
* Majority of customers prefer single-installment payments
* Low adoption of long-term installment plans

---

## Key Insights

* E-commerce demand shows strong growth and seasonality
* Revenue is concentrated in a few high-performing states
* Logistics efficiency varies significantly by region
* Delivery delays highlight operational improvement areas
* Customer payment behavior favors convenience and immediacy
